## Strategic Insights

### Insight: Solo PMs Have No Bandwidth for Structured CompInt — But They Still Do It Manually
- **Quote:** "I was the one who was handling them. Product manager, team, use designing team and then marketing team as well... within this 8 month of time frame I built nearly 4 plus product which I didn't had any time to go on the market strategy."
- **Implication for Kompete:** Resource-constrained PMs at early-stage companies represent a real but different ICP from Kompete's stated target ($5–200M ARR). They feel the pain acutely but likely cannot justify or fund a $2–5K/yr tool on their own — they need either a freemium/self-serve entry or to be accessed through the company buyer, not the individual PM.

### Insight: Customer Review Signals Are Trusted; Synthesized/AI Narratives May Not Be
- **Quote:** "I don't believe influencer marketing and so on so thing they are paid promotion and so I do believe only in feedbacks of customers."
- **Implication for Kompete:** The AI-synthesized competitor messaging analysis (website headline tracking, content monitoring) may face skepticism from review-centric PMs. Kompete should anchor credibility in raw review data (G2, Capterra, Google) and show the source, not just the AI summary — "show your work" to build trust.

### Insight: GTM Signal Tracking Has Low Perceived Urgency for Junior/Solo PMs
- **Quote:** "No, not into the GDM strategy... I didn't had any time to go on the market strategy and launching strategy and so on."
- **Implication for Kompete:** Hiring signals, launch alerts, and content monitoring — while powerful — may be low-resonance features for junior PM personas. Kompete's GTM story likely needs to be led by customer/review intelligence for this segment, and the deeper signals positioned as upgrades for more strategic buyers (VP Product, PMM).

### Insight: The EdTech Competitive Analysis Use Case Is Hyper-Informal and Cohort Revenue Is Surprisingly Trackable
- **Quote:** "The Air Tribe community was earning nearly like 3.5 crore for a single cohort base which goes like nearly four months. In terms of product space they were earning nearly like 15 lakhs per cohort."
- **Implication for Kompete:** Even in non-SaaS markets (EdTech cohort programs), practitioners are manually estimating competitor revenue from public signals. This validates demand for structured competitor revenue/traction tracking beyond just features and messaging — a potential expansion angle.
