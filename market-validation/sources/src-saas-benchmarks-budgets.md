## Key data points

### Budget context ($5-50M ARR)
- Total S&M spending median: **31% of ARR** for $5-20M companies; **40%** for $20-50M
- Sales costs median: **13% of ARR** (up from 10.5% prior year)
- Marketing costs median: **8% of ARR**
- Product marketing/branding (includes sales enablement, CI): **8-10% of marketing budget**
- Sales-Led Growth companies allocate **~44% of marketing budget** to programs including enablement, ABM, field events
- Median company spends **$2.00 to acquire $1.00 of new ARR**
- CI-specific: NOT a separate line item; embedded in marketing, sales enablement, or G&A

### CI tool pricing context
- Klue / Crayon: **$20K-$40K/year** (mid-market); **$40-$80K/year** (enterprise)
- Smaller tools (Anterion etc): **$500-$2,000/month**
- Hidden cost of manual CI: **$20K-$39K/year** in PMM time (5-10 hrs/week at $75-150/hr)

### Churn benchmarks
- B2B SaaS average: **~3.5% monthly** (2.6% voluntary, 0.8% involuntary) = **~35% annual**
- SMBs: **3-7% monthly** (31-58% annual)
- Enterprise: **1-2% annual**
- "Competition — users finding better alternatives elsewhere" cited as top churn driver

### Startup failure from competition
- **20% of startups fail** because they are outcompeted (LEANFoundry)

### When CI function emerges
- Series B onward: formal PMM function typically emerges
- Informal tracking "breaks down the moment you close a Series A"
- **57% of enterprise orgs** have at least 1 full-time win-loss employee
- Only **37% of small companies** do
