## Key data points

### Vendor proliferation
- Martech: 300 → **15,384 solutions** in 15 years (100x growth). AI added 1,200 new vendors in 2025.
- Global SaaS companies: 30,000 (2023) → **70,000+ (2024)** — 133% increase in one year
- B2B buyers evaluate average **4.5 vendors per purchase**, with buying committees generating up to **4,000 touchpoints**

### AI lowering barriers
- **64% of SaaS CEOs** say generative AI is lowering barriers to entry; another 20% expect it soon
- Small teams creating "credible, competitive products in weeks, not months"
- "Markets that once supported only a handful of dominant vendors now seeing dozens of viable challengers"

### Sales cycle data
- Sales cycles increased **~13%** on average (Tunguz 2024 GTM Survey) — consistent with more competitive evaluations
- AI-referenced deals: **72% of all SaaS transactions in 2025** (12x since 2018)

## Verdict
STRONG evidence that competition is intensifying structurally. Not cyclical. AI is compressing time-to-build and increasing vendor count across every category.
