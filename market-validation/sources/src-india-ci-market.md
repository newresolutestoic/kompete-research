## India-based CI players

### Contify (Gurgaon, India)
- Founded 2009
- **BOOTSTRAPPED** — no VC funding
- Revenue: $8.2M (2024), up from $5.5M (2023) — 49% YoY growth
- 50 customers, 325 employees
- India = 11.54% of customer base (6 companies). Rest is US/global.
- Launched "Athena" agentic AI engine (July 2025)
- This is the ONLY notable India-headquartered CI platform

### Clootrack (India)

## India-specific observations
- No dedicated India CI market sizing from any major analyst
- No India-focused CI VC investment found
- Indian B2B SaaS companies are likely doing CI manually (spreadsheets, ChatGPT, PMM time)
- Opportunities identified in technology, e-commerce, finance sectors
- Willingness-to-pay problem likely MORE severe than US

## Implication for Kompete
- India is white space — Contify is bootstrapped and small
- But the absence of VC funding suggests the market isn't yet proven for venture-scale outcomes
- Pricing must be dramatically different: $2-5K/year or usage-based vs $20-40K for US market
- India as secondary market (design partners, testimonials) makes sense. India as primary market is risky.
