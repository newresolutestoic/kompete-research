## Why this source matters
When operators say "process is the fulcrum," they often mean sales process (MEDDPICC, Challenger, SPIN). This source proves that the most respected enterprise sales process REQUIRES competitive intelligence as a core element.

## Key data points
- In MEDDPICC, the **second "C" stands explicitly for Competition**
- Competition is defined as "any person, vendor, or initiative competing for the same funds or resources"
- Broadens beyond direct rivals to include anything competing for the prospect's budget
- Multiple MEDDPICC elements force competitive consideration:
  - **Metrics:** Comparative measure of benefits vs. competition
  - **Decision Criteria:** Forces sellers to consider competitive positioning
  - **Champion:** Most reliable source of competitive intel (who else is being evaluated)

## Direct implication
- You cannot execute MEDDPICC without competitive intelligence
- "Process vs CI" is a false choice — CI is a required INPUT to the process
- Companies saying "we focus on process" while running MEDDPICC are already admitting they need CI
