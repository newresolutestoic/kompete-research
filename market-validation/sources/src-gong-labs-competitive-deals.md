## Why this source matters
Gong has the largest dataset of actual B2B sales conversations. Their analysis shows what ACTUALLY correlates with winning deals, not what people say correlates.

## Key data points
- Customers who **mention competitors early** in the sales cycle are **49% more likely to close** than those who don't mention competitors at all
- Early competitive mention increases odds of winning an enterprise deal by **32%**
- **Late-stage competitor mentions** correlate with LOWER win rates + smaller deal sizes (reps discount to survive)
- **Multi-threading** boosts win rates by **130%** in deals over $50K (1.8M opportunities)
- Selling teams for closed-won deals are **67% larger** than for lost deals
- Gong AI is **21% more precise** than reps in predicting winning deals by week 4

## Direct implications
- Early competitive awareness = higher win rates. Late awareness = discounting + losing.
- The 47% of reps who don't spot competitors until negotiation (Klue data) are exactly the ones who end up discounting and losing.
- Competitive prep is not separate from deal execution — it IS deal execution.
