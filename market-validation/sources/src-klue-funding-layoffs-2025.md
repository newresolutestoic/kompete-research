## Key data points

### Funding
- Total raised: ~$81M (Seed $4M, Series A ~$15M, Series B $62M)
- Series B led by **Tiger Global** (Dec 2021), with **Salesforce Ventures**
- Tiger Global partner: "demand for competitive intelligence is exploding"
- Salesforce Ventures: "95% of companies invest in their compete function"

### Acquisitions (3 in 2.5 years)
- Jan 2023: DoubleCheck Research (win-loss analysis) — "low-double-digit millions"
- Mar 2025: Goldpan (AI-powered win-loss)
- Sep 2025: Ignition (agentic AI for product marketers)

### BEAR SIGNAL — June 2025 Layoffs
- **40% of workforce laid off** (~85 employees)
- **Burned $28M since 2023 to grow revenue by only $2.5M**
- Growth revised to **low double-digit** (from much higher)
- CEO Jason Smith: "There is ambiguity about what people will pay for"
- **Losing deals to ChatGPT and internal AI experimentation**
- Revenue estimated: $25-50M CAD range
- Targeting profitability by Q4 2025

## Why this matters
- Simultaneously the biggest bear AND bull signal for CI
- Bear: $81M raised, Tiger Global backed, and still struggling to grow
- Bull: proves the PRE-AI approach hit a ceiling — window for AI-native disruption
- CEO confirming ChatGPT substitution validates that form factor must change
