## Why this source matters
Largest recent survey on how competitive dynamics affect revenue. N=313 revenue leaders. Provides specific percentages on deal losses, rep preparedness, and CS vulnerability.

## Key data points
- **21% of deals** lost directly to competitors (average estimate across respondents)
- **33% of those competitive losses are winnable** = ~7% of ALL deals are recoverable
- **47% of reps** spot competitors only at negotiation stage or later (too late to adjust)
- **13%** don't know which competitor was involved even after deal closes
- **73% of leaders** confident reps know key differentiators, but **only 30%** can demonstrate them
- **76%** see greater separation between top and bottom performers in competitive markets
- Orgs NOT using win-loss are **86% more likely to misunderstand** competitor strategies
- Orgs NOT using win-loss are **2x more likely to struggle** differentiating
- **Only 40%** confident CS teams can detect competitor presence in renewals
- **38% of revenue leaders** identify customer retention as the most impactful revenue stream

## Direct quotes
- "Improving customer retention is the most overlooked opportunity for current competitive enablement programs to impact revenue"
