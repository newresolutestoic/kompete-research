## Why this source matters
Longest-running annual CI report (8 editions). Provides trend data over time and specific effectiveness metrics tied to CI practices.

## Key data points
- **68% of B2B sales deals** involve at least one direct competitor
- Average sales team rates competitive preparedness at **3.8 out of 10**
- Competitive preparedness gap costs organizations **$2-$10 million/year** in winnable deals
- **55%** of companies seeing MORE competitive opportunities than previous year
- **52%** of compete programs lack a sales executive sponsor
- **44%** of companies lack competitor visibility within their CRM
- Less than **1/3** of compete programs engage with sales daily or weekly

### High-impact practice correlations
- Daily sales enablement with CI: **84% increase** in competitive sales effectiveness
- Sales exec sponsor for compete program: **76% boost** in effectiveness
- Conversational intelligence tracking competitor mentions: **82% lift** in win rates
- Dedicated compete platforms: **79% increase** in effectiveness
- **Battle cards updated monthly: up to 59% win rate lift**

### 2024 Report (supplementary)
- **65%** of sales opportunities are competitive for average software companies
- **79%** of CI professionals arm sales teams with battlecards
- **58%** report difficulty keeping battlecards updated
- **25%** of CI leaders already use AI tools; **56%** plan to adopt
