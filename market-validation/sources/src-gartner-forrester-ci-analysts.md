## CRITICAL FINDING: Gartner published INAUGURAL MQ in April 2026

This is brand new — the category previously only had a "Market Guide." Upgrade to full MQ signals Gartner believes the market has matured enough for formal evaluation.

### Category renaming
- "Competitive and Market Intelligence Tools" → "Competitive and Market Intelligence **Platforms**"
- Signals shift from point solution to horizontal enterprise platform

### 2026 Gartner MQ Leaders
- **AlphaSense** — Highest on BOTH axes. $500M+ ARR, $4B valuation, 7,000+ enterprise customers (90% of S&P 100)
- **Northern Light** — Also Leader. ~$15.8M revenue, 58 employees, $23M investment from LoneTree Capital

### Visionaries
- **Market Logic Software**

### Notable: Klue is NOT a Leader
- Klue named "Strong Performer" in Forrester Wave Q4 2024 — not a Leader
- Forrester Leader: **Valona Intelligence** (Finnish, 350K+ daily users, serves Fortune 500 since 1999)

### Gartner prediction
- By 2026, **40% of technology and service providers** will use commercial CI tools, up from ~10%

## THESIS-CHANGING INSIGHT

The CI market is BIFURCATING:
1. **Enterprise market intelligence platforms** (AlphaSense $500M ARR, Valona, Northern Light) — serving strategy/finance teams, $4B+ valuations possible
2. **Sales-enablement CI tools** (Klue ~$25-50M, Crayon ~$25-50M) — serving PMM/sales, ceiling appears to be ~$50M ARR

AlphaSense proves CI CAN scale massively. But it serves a different buyer (finance/strategy) with a different use case (market research). The sales-enablement CI subcategory has NO company above ~$50M ARR.
