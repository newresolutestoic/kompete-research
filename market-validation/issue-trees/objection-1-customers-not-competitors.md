# Issue Tree: "Great companies focus on customers, not competitors"

**Root question:** Is competitive awareness necessary, or is customer obsession sufficient?

## Dimension 1.1: Strategic Theory
> Do the frameworks people cite actually reject CI?

| Claim | What it actually says | Verdict | Source |
|-------|----------------------|---------|--------|
| "Porter says focus on your value chain" | Five Forces IS about competitive structure | Misread | [src-strategic-theory](../sources/src-strategic-theory-porter-christensen-blueocean.md) |
| "Christensen says listen to customers" | Companies die from OVER-focusing on current customers | Says the opposite | [src-strategic-theory](../sources/src-strategic-theory-porter-christensen-blueocean.md) |
| "Blue Ocean says make competition irrelevant" | Strategy Canvas requires mapping AGAINST competitors | Output ≠ input | [src-strategic-theory](../sources/src-strategic-theory-porter-christensen-blueocean.md) |
| "Bezos says customer obsessed not competitor obsessed" | Amazon has 310+ CI roles, crawlers on competitor sites | Positioning ≠ operations | [src-amazon-ci](../sources/src-amazon-ci-operations.md) |

**Dimension verdict:** The objection misreads every framework it claims to cite.

## Dimension 1.4: False Dichotomy Test
> Is it customers OR competitors, or customers AND competitors?

| Evidence | Number | Source |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Revenue leaders: retention = #1 revenue stream | 38% | [src-klue](../sources/src-klue-competitive-revenue-gap-2025.md) |
| CS teams that can detect competitor in renewals | Only 40% | [src-klue](../sources/src-klue-competitive-revenue-gap-2025.md) |
| Competitive awareness improves customer outcomes | RCT: 8% more customers | [src-management-science](../sources/src-management-science-kim-2025.md) |

**Dimension verdict:** False dichotomy. CI IS customer focus. Your customer is being poached and you don't know.

---

## OBJECTION 1 OVERALL VERDICT

**Wrong on theory, wrong on data, wrong on revealed behavior, based on a false dichotomy.**
Competitive awareness and customer focus are complementary, not competing.
The correct pitch dissolves the dichotomy: "We watch competitors SO THAT you serve customers better."
